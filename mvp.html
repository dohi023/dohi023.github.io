#34 Giannis Antetokounmpo - Most Valuable Player 2018-19

Giannis Antetokounmpo has 4 brothers, and 2 of them are currently competing in the NBA.
One, Thanasis Antetokounmpo, plays for the same team as Giannis, the Milwaukee Bucks. The other, Kostas Antetokounmpo, plays for the Los Angeles Lakers.
Giannis is from Greece, and is well known for his body being a freak of nature. For this reason, many refer to him as "the Greek Freek".
