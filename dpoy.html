Rudy Gobert - Defensive Player of the Year 2018-19

Rudy Gobert is well known for his defence inside the key. He has won this same award (DPOY)
in the 2017-18 season, which makes him a 2 time DPOY winner. His height is listed at 2.16 meters
and his wingspan is listed at 2.36 meters.
Gobert is currently playing for the Utah Jazz.
