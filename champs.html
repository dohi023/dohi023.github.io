Toronto Raptors - Championship Title 2018-19

The Toronto Raptors is the only Canadian team in the National Basketball Association.
The Toronto Raptors won their very first Championship Title in the 2018-2019 NBA Season, while number 2, Kawhi Leonard, won Finals Most Valuable Player.
Kawhi Leonard averaged 26.6 points, 7.5 rebounds, 3.3 steals, 0.4 blocks, and 2.0 turnovers per game during the 2018-2019 regular season.
Currently, he is playing for the Los Angeles Clippers.
